Mission

Booker Academy Library faces the following problems: manual locating and knowing availability of the books. Manual in knowing the list of employee. Less efficient and Time consuming. Unmanageable record and list of books. The Librarian has delayed response to the inquiry. The librarian needs enough time to track down the borrowed book.

Vision

My project will integrate book circulation system in the library and specific shelves to be placed/shelved which is on certain territory/arrangement of the library. It will implement easy to use and understand digital book procurement flow in the library.
